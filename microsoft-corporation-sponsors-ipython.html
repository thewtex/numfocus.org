Microsoft Corporation sponsors IPython

Fri 27 September 2013

NumFOCUS and IPython are thrilled to announce that in August 2013, Microsoft made a donation of $100,000 to sponsor IPython’s continued development. Microsoft and IPython have had a fruitful collaboration, dating back to 2009, involving Microsoft’s Windows HPC Server, Python Tools for Visual Studio, and an Azure-based workflow. The response from the IPython team is posted in their announcement.

NumFOCUS was founded, in part, to foster this type of collaboration between open source scientific tools and companies who value this shared computational infrastructure. For information on how your company can support NumFOCUS and its affiliated projects, please our donations page or contact us directly at info@numfocus.org.
